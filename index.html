Our Mission

Power lies in togetherness. We as an individual can stand against odds always but as an amalgamation we stand stronger. With a very competent team and strong determination, E. A. S. T by E-Cell, VSSUT envisions to promote enterpreneurial spirits and fabricate the startup culture in the state of Odisha and in the whole nation. With mutual understanding and cooperation, we can definitely make this programme a grand success and contribute towards country's development.

  • Collaborate all E-Cell's of the Country under EAST.
  • Provide support to each others initiative.
  • Help each other spread awareness of startup culture.
